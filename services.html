Our Products

Currently Chidi-TS do business in subsystems required for pulsed, mono-pulsed, CW, FMCW, DBF/DOA and interferometry radars. Based on customer requirement and the type of radar application, we design, develop and provide the following products.

  • Radar subsystems


RF trans receiver

HF, VHF, UHF, L, S, C bands

Digital Receiver

Baseband extraction from modulated RF/IF analog signals

Radar Controller

System health monitoring, synchronized operations

Signal Processor

Data processor

Timing Controller Unit
